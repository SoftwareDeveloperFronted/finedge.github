Comprehensive Loan Eligibility Criteria

Basic Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 21 - 60 years
  • Employment: At least 2 years of stable employment or business ownership
  • Income: Minimum ₹25,000 monthly
  • Credit Score: Minimum 600
  • Existing Loans: No more than 2 active loans
  • Documents Required: Aadhar, PAN, Salary slips, Bank statements, Address proof

Why These Criteria Matter

These eligibility criteria are designed to ensure responsible lending and minimize default risk. A good credit score reflects timely repayments in the past and financial discipline. Stable employment indicates regular income, which is essential for repayment ability. Proper documentation helps in preventing fraud and verifying identity.

Tips to Improve Loan Eligibility

  • Maintain a high credit score by paying bills and EMIs on time.
  • Reduce existing debts before applying for a new loan.
  • Ensure all documents are up-to-date and correctly filled.
  • Increase your monthly income by additional sources or side business.
